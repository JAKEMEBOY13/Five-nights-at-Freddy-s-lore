Five Nights at Freddy's Lore Timeline

A fan-friendly chronology of major FNaF events based on long-running MatPat/Game Theory interpretations released before Matthew Patrick's YouTube retirement.

Disclaimer: This is a theory-driven timeline (not an official canon transcript) and condenses years of videos and updates.

How to Read This Timeline

  • High confidence recurring points across many theories
  • Medium confidence likely but debated interpretation
  • Low confidence speculative or frequently revised ideas
1970s–Early 1980s

Fredbear's Family Diner Begins

Henry Emily and William Afton launch an early animatronic venue. This sets up the partnership that later fractures into tragedy.

Theory confidence: High

1983

The Crying Child & the Bite

MatPat often frames the FNaF 4 minigames as the Afton household tragedy: a younger Afton son is bitten during a birthday party, sparking guilt and a family collapse.

Theory confidence: High

Mid-1980s

Missing Children Incident

Children disappear after being lured by a figure associated with the Spring Bonnie suit. Their spirits are believed to haunt core animatronics, driving the series' supernatural thread.

Theory confidence: High

1987

FNaF 2 Location & The Bite of '87

A newer Freddy Fazbear's Pizza opens with toy animatronics, facial recognition, and heightened security. A severe bite incident helps shut this era down.

Theory confidence: Medium

Early 1990s

FNaF 1 Night Guard Era

The original game events unfold in a struggling pizzeria. Theories often connect Mike Schmidt to Michael Afton and treat this period as part of his long attempt to undo his father's harm.

Theory confidence: High

Post-Closure

Afton Becomes Springtrap

While dismantling haunted animatronics, William is cornered and trapped in a springlock suit. This transformation into Springtrap anchors many later events.

Theory confidence: High

~30 Years Later

Fazbear's Fright (FNaF 3)

A horror attraction built from Fazbear relics uncovers Springtrap. Fire consumes the attraction, but Afton appears to survive.

Theory confidence: High

After Sister Location

Michael's Search & Remnant Experiments

Game Theory frequently links Sister Location to Afton's underground experiments and introduces remnant as a key mechanism for possession and persistence.

Theory confidence: Medium

FNaF 6 (Pizzeria Simulator)

Henry's Fire Plan

Henry gathers remaining haunted entities in one location and burns the building, aiming to end the cycle and free trapped souls.

Theory confidence: High

VR Era onward

Glitchtrap, Vanny & the Mega Pizzaplex

The franchise pivots into digital hauntings. MatPat's late-era theories explore whether Afton survives as code, how Vanessa/Vanny is manipulated, and how Security Breach + Ruin reshuffle identities.

Theory confidence: Medium
